Background

The story so far

I started my career at KPMG as a Certified Public Accountant — learning to see every business through the lens of numbers, risk, and consequence. That rigor has shaped everything I've done since.

I pivoted into tech and product management, bringing something most PMs don't have: genuine financial depth. I've shipped products at FreeWheel (Comcast's ad tech platform) and currently drive product strategy at IQVIA, one of the world's largest healthcare data and analytics companies.

I built the tool on this page because I believe everyday people deserve clear, judgment-free financial thinking — the kind you'd get from a smart friend who happens to be a CPA.
